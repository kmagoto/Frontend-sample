Rottweiler

Robust and powerful, the Rottweiler dog breed is happiest when given a job to perform.

  • Temperament: Steady, Obedient, Calm
  • Weight: 35-60 kg
  • Height: 56-69 cm
  • Colors: Black, Tan, Mahogany

Rottweilers are people dogs and are affectionate and loyal toward their families. They are great family members and are excellent guard dogs as well. They tend to follow their favorite person from room to room, so they are always within eyesight.

Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin nakes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average Length: 11 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers. They reach about 11 cm in length, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.

Dolphin

Celebrated and known for their playful behavior, dolphins are highly intelligent creatures.

  • Lifespan: 10-60 years
  • Mass: 3000-4000 kg
  • Speed: 56-60 km/h
  • Gestation period: 15-18 months

Dolphins are also one of the few species to pass the self-awareness test, where they can identify themselves in a mirror. On top of that, they can actually recognize their own reflection much earlier on in their lives than other animals, and that includes humans.
